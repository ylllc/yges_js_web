# Additional Methods

-----
## start()

the Engine is started.  
and cannot exit until call stop() or shutdown()  

-----
## stop()

stop the Engine.  
and all procedures are aborted.  
can start() again.  

-----
## shutdown()

stop the Engine.  
and all procedures are aborted.  
no longer restart it.
